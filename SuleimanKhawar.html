When Suleimon was a young young boy, he found that he was dissociated from society. He would day dream all day rather than play with the other kids. Being in his mind all the time, he became a rather introspective person. He would always building worlds within his own imagination. Time went by, and Suleimon found himself graduating highschool. After much thought, he knew what he wanted to do in the future. He decided he wanted to program, so he could bring his imagination to life.
